Module 1-3 Reflection

Building positive relationships is an important task in our daily lives; social networking through communication and relationships is the most effective way for people to be successful at work. Building a positive relationship with our subordinates creates an environment in which your subordinates are willing to work and put forth the effort necessary to be successful. Effective communication is essential for establishing positive relationships. With all of your subordinates, superiors, and peers, effective communication conveys your goals and paths to those goals. Effective face-to-face communication has numerous advantages. It not only helps individuals build relationships, but it also helps individuals understand the task at hand as well as the desired outcomes of those tasks.

This lesson taught me that we all deserve to develop relationships with those in our lives. What matters most are the memories and friendships we have with others, regardless of how many people we have relationships with. This program, on the other hand, educated me about the considerations that people make when they need to interact with others. Other than that, this module discusses the significance of maintaining a personal contact with those near to us. Individually, daily social interaction is already a component of our existence. This engagement with others, particularly those with whom we create bonds, aids in the improvement and development of our personalities and overall selves. It helps us improve our lives by sharing their testimonies and personal experiences with us, because learning from their experiences gives us with life lessons that can lead and maintain our everyday lives. If we are having such challenges in our life, for example, we can turn to the people with whom we have a personal relationship for support as well as guidance on how to overcome the situation. Additionally, the personal contact we have with ourfamily gives us with the love and support we need, enriching our lives because our family is always there for us when we are depressed.

To summarize, our companions, like our family, are the people we turn to when we aren't feeling well; they are the ones who help us forget about our problems and doubts since, like family, they can cheer us up and provide everlasting support. It's also true in our personal connections, because romantic partnerships, especially healthy ones, generate a lot of inspiration and drive, prompting people to strive for more. As a result, our friends, spouses, and family members play a significant role in preventing stress and pressures in our lives.
